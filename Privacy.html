Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your username, email address, and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store your password itself). We also store the avatar you pick for your profile.

Chat messages

Messages sent in chat rooms are visible to everyone in that room and are kept temporarily on the server to show recent history to new joiners.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, and we don't share your email with third parties.

Contact

Questions about your data? Reach out via our YouTube channel.
