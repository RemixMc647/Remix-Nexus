About 𝕽𝖊𝖒𝖎𝖝 𝕹𝖊𝖝𝖚𝖘

Last updated: July 2026

𝕽𝖊𝖒𝖎𝖝 𝕹𝖊𝖝𝖚𝖘 (RemixMC) is a free, community-run gaming society built for players who'd rather squad up than grind alone. Whether you're into Minecraft, Roblox, Fortnite, Call of Duty, Free Fire, PUBG, GTA, or Among Us, RemixMC gives you a live room to talk in, real people to play with, and a place to keep up with what the community is doing.

What you'll find here

  • Live chat rooms — jump into a room for your favorite game and talk in real time.
  • Contacts & private messages — keep talking one-on-one with people you've met in a room.
  • Live & Trending — the latest uploads from the RemixMC YouTube channel and community highlights.
  • Search — find a squad room by game name in a couple of keystrokes.
  • Profiles — a simple account and avatar system so people recognize you across rooms.

Good to know

  • Chat messages are temporary — to keep rooms fast and clutter-free, every message you send automatically disappears after 3 days.
  • Video uploads are capped at 30 minutes — clips can run up to 30 minutes long; anything longer won't be accepted.
  • Enable your mic and notifications on browser — using RemixMC in a browser? Head into your browser's site settings and allow microphone access to send voice notes, and allow notifications so you never miss a reply.

Our approach

RemixMC is built to stay lightweight and free to join. There's no pay-to-win, no locked chat rooms, and no barrier between you and the next match. We do run advertising on this site to help cover hosting and development costs, and we're upfront about that — see our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for the details.

Get in touch

Got feedback, found a bug, or want to suggest a new squad room? Head over to our Contact page — we read every message.
